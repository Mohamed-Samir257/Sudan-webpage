Random Facts.

Sudan is the home of over 200 pyatmids, more than twice the number found in Egypt.
The Bule Nile and White Nile rivers converge in Khartoum, The Capital of Sudan
Sudan is a culturally divers country with over 500 ethic groups and 400 languages spoken
Sudan was once part of the Kingdom of Kush, a powerful civilization that flourished along the Nile River for over 1,000 years
